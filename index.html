About Us. Extended Lifecare, Beyond Boundaries

Zylo Pharma Private Limited is an Indian based pharmaceuticals company that strives to be among the top pharmaceutical companies in India. It's aim is to deliver top of the class medical services across the globe, by delivering the innovative, branded and generic medicines trusted and approved by healthcare professionals and patients across the world.

With high quality, precision and a thought to create a difference, that is how Zylo Pharma strives to make a difference. The Head Office is situated in New Delhi and the on process manufacturing plant is situated in Gurugram,Haryana.

Zylo Pharma Private Limited, is backed by a great deal of hardwork and countless man-hours both skilled and enthusiast that strive to make a difference. We starve to create value for our customers by saving them their money and more importantly, time.
